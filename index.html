Background

Who I am.

I'm a founder and designer based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, building software products that take AI seriously — not as a buzzword, but as a lever for changing how people think and work.

My work sits at the intersection of cognitive tools, product design, and AI. I care about interfaces that respect the user's attention, software that coaches rather than replaces, and design systems with real opinions. I'm obsessive about typography.

Before going deep on commercial products, I co-built BridgeAI — a social impact venture for young people in East Africa. I think a lot about philosophy, faith, and what it means to build things that matter.
